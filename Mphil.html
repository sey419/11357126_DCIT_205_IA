MPhil / MSc Programmes

The Department of Computer Science at the University of Ghana has two masterâ€™s programmes in Computer Science: a Master of Science (MSc.) and a Master of Philosophy (MPhil.). The MSc. programme is a one-year (weekend/evening) programme that involves a capstone project. The MPhil. programme is a two-year (full-time) programme that consists of a taught course in the first year and a research and dissertation in the second year. Both programmes are designed to train current and future computer scientists who can study either full-time or part-time. The programmes aim to provide students with a broad and deep knowledge of theoretical computer science and advanced technical skills to use science and technology as a powerful tool to solve complex and challenging problems in the global society. The programmes also expose students to practical topics such as Advanced Databases, Advanced Data Structures and Algorithms and Networks.
