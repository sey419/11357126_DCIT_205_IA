PhD Programmes

The doctoral programme is a full-time research programme that lasts for four years. It requires a high level of dedication from the students. Students need to live near the University and be fully involved in the programme. The postgraduate committee will help the Ph.D. students to design a personalized programme of study that includes a major area and at least two minor areas of specialization. The department also organizes regular seminars where students and faculty discuss and evaluate research in the field. Students are expected to join at most one research lab and take part in the research activities of the group. The department has four research areas: Intelligent Space and Machine Learning, Data Science/Big Data Analytics, Networking and Communications Engineering, and Cyber Security.
